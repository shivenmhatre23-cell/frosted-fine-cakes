Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to some of the most common questions about ordering your cake from Frosted & Fine Cakes.

Do you make custom cakes?

Absolutely! We create custom cakes tailored to your theme, colours, occasion, and personal preferences. Share your ideas, and we'll bring them to life.

How early should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 3–5 days in advance. Designer and custom cakes may require additional time depending on their complexity.

Do you offer eggless cakes?

Yes! All of our flavours are available in eggless options. Simply let us know your preference while placing your order.

Do you provide home delivery?

Delivery and pickup options are available depending on your location. Contact us on WhatsApp for delivery availability and charges.

Important Order Information

Please review the following information before placing your order.

📍 Dombivli Orders Only

We currently accept orders only within Dombivli and nearby serviceable locations.

📅 Advance Booking

Kindly place your order in advance to ensure availability, especially during weekends and festive seasons.

💳 Advance Payment

A partial or full advance payment may be required to confirm customised and premium cake orders.

🚚 Delivery

Delivery charges may apply depending on your location and preferred delivery time.

🎨 Customisation

Colours, decorations and overall design may vary slightly as every cake is handcrafted.

✨ Additional Design Charges

Premium decorations such as fondant work, edible toppers, handcrafted flowers, metallic finishes and other advanced customisations are charged separately in addition to the base cake price.
